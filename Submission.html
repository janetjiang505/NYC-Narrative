How To Post Your Story:

To submit your story email us at Nycnarrative@gmail.com! We choose top stories to be featured each week. Just send your age, borough, and the race that you identify with. First names are optional. We can't wait to receive your stories!

Story Requirements:

Your story should be true and something that you're comfortable sharing with the internet. Most importantly, have fun with your story!

Requirements to post a story:

You must be between the ages of 13 to 19. This is to ensure a safe community for our users.

Have any feedback?

Please email us! Shoot an email to Nycnarrative@gmail.com! We'd love to hear any opinions or advice!
